# ⚡ FORTRESS FACT-CHECK — Executive Summary

## 30-Second Pitch

**Fortress Fact-Check is PolitiFact for stock tips.**

When a financial influencer says "This stock has 30% CAGR," users paste the ticker into Fortress, which fact-checks the claim in 5 seconds using real financial data.

Users pay $10/mo for unlimited checks. Platforms (Zerodha, moomoo) pay for integration. Advisors white-label it. Institutions buy APIs.
