## Risk Mitigation

### **Technical Risks**
| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-----------|
| Data accuracy | High | Spot-check 20 stocks weekly, manual verification |
| API downtime | High | 99.9% SLA, redundant APIs, circuit breakers |
| Slow performance | Medium | Cache everything, CDN for static, lazy load |

### **Business Risks**
| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-----------|
| Influencers fight back | Medium | Frame as "validation", not "exposure" |
| Competitors copy | Medium | First-mover + data + network effects |
| Regulatory (financial advice) | High | Clear disclaimers, "educational only" |
| Low adoption | Medium | Partner with platforms (Zerodha), not just users |

### **Legal Risks**
| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-----------|
| Defamation | Low | Stick to facts, cite sources, no opinions |
| Financial advice | Medium | Disclaimers on every page, no recommendations |
| Data privacy | Low | No personal data collected (stateless MVP) |
