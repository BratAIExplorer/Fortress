How to use this satellite sleeve

  • Size: Total satellite exposure capped at ~10% of the MYR 20,000 portfolio (~MYR 2,000). This is speculative money you can afford to see drop 30-40% — it does not replace the diversified core built earlier.
  • Don't stack duplicates: SMH, SOXX, FTXL, CHPS, EWT, and 0050 mostly hold the same 15-25 stocks. Owning several is concentration, not diversification. Pick SMH (broadest) and/or the Japan equipment ETF (genuinely different holdings) instead of all of them.
  • Entry discipline: After 60-300%+ one-year moves, don't lump-sum buy at the top. Split any new money into 2-3 tranches over 6-10 weeks.
  • Forward return expectation: Base rates say extreme recent returns are far more likely to mean-revert than repeat. Treat "another 70-80% in a year" as the low-probability tail case, not the plan.
  • Exit rule: Set a hard stop (e.g. −15% to −20% from your entry, or from the recent 52-week high) on the Korea position specifically — leveraged flows there are already unwinding.
  • Review cadence: Check this table monthly, not daily. Momentum sleeves need less frequent but more decisive action (trim winners, cut losers) rather than constant tinkering.
