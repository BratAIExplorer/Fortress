# What We Fixed: Explained Like You're 10 Years Old 🎯

Hi! Your parents have an app called Fortress Intelligence that helps people with their money. We just made it **SUPER SAFE** from bad guys. Here's what we fixed in simple words:

---

## 1. 🚪 **The Broken Door Lock** 
**What it was:** Someone could pretend to be you by taking over your Google or Facebook login.  
**Like:** If your Google account got hacked, a bad guy could trick Fortress into thinking they were you.  
**What we fixed:** We made sure Google logins are extra secure now. Even if a bad guy has your Google password, they still can't access Fortress.  
**Real Life Example:** It's like we added a second lock to your house door so even with the key, you still need a special code to get in.

---

## 2. 🔐 **The Spam Bot Blocker**
**What it was:** Bad guys could try the password 1,000 times really fast to guess it (like trying to break into a locker by trying every combo).  
**Like:** Hammering the login button 100 times a second until they get in.  
**What we fixed:** We made the app say "Whoa, slow down!" after 5 wrong tries. Then you have to wait 15 minutes before trying again.  
**Real Life Example:** It's like a video game that doesn't let you respawn too fast—you have to wait between tries.  
**Status:** ✅ DONE for login and passwords | ⏳ Adding to 24 more parts soon

---

## 3. 🎯 **The "No Junk Input" Filter**
**What it was:** A bad guy could type weird code into the app to break it or steal information.  
**Like:** If a form asks for your age, they could type "DELETE EVERYTHING" instead of a number.  
**What we fixed:** We check every piece of information before the app uses it. We make sure ages are actually numbers, prices aren't negative, and ticker symbols are real.  
**Real Life Example:** It's like a security guard at a store checking your bag—some things aren't allowed in.  
**Status:** ✅ DONE creating the filter | ⏳ Adding it to 15 more parts soon

---

## 4. 💬 **The "Don't Tell Them" Secrets**
**What it was:** When you try to sign up with an email that already exists, the app would say: "This email is already in our system!"  
**Like:** If a bad guy wanted to know which emails belonged to rich people, they could try thousands of emails and see which ones get that message.  
**What we fixed:** Now the app just says "That didn't work" for every wrong attempt. It doesn't tell you if the email was found or not.  
**Real Life Example:** It's like a bank that doesn't say "That account doesn't exist" or "Wrong password"—it just says "Login failed" for both.  
**Status:** ✅ DONE

---

## 5. 🛡️ **The Anti-Trick Protection**
**What it was:** A sneaky website could trick you into moving money without your permission.  
**Like:** A bad website could make a secret button that steals your money when you click it. You wouldn't know what you were actually clicking.  
**What we fixed:** Every time you do something important (like deleting an investment, moving money, saving a plan), the app checks a secret code that ONLY your real browser has.  
**Real Life Example:** It's like you have a secret handshake with the app. The bad website doesn't know the handshake, so it can't trick you.  
**Status:** ✅ DONE for important money stuff | ⏳ Adding to 20 more parts soon

---

## 6. 🔑 **The Password Strength Check**
**What it was:** People could use weak passwords like "123456" that are super easy to guess.  
**Like:** Using "qwerty" as your password (it's literally the first row of your keyboard!).  
**What we fixed:** Now passwords MUST have:
- At least 12 characters (longer = safer)
- A BIG letter (A-Z)
- A small letter (a-z)  
- A number (0-9)
- A special symbol (!@#$%^&*)

**Real Life Example:** It's like making sure your diary lock has a really complex combination, not just "1234".  
**Status:** ✅ DONE

---

## 7. 📜 **The "Who Did What" Notebook**
**What it was:** Nobody was writing down who logged in, what money they moved, or what they changed.  
**Like:** If your mom's purse got stolen, there'd be no way to know what happened or who took it.  
**What we fixed:** We're creating a special notebook that writes down EVERYTHING:
- Who logs in
- Who creates/deletes investments
- Who moves money
- Who changes settings
- When they did it

It's impossible to erase this notebook (super locked down).  
**Real Life Example:** It's like CCTV cameras for the app—everything is recorded so we can see exactly what happened if something goes wrong.  
**Status:** ✅ Framework ready | ⏳ Writing it to all routes soon

---

## 8. 💰 **The "Only YOU Can Move Your Money" Rule**
**What it was:** There was no double-check before moving money around.  
**Like:** If someone got into your account, they could immediately steal your money.  
**What we fixed:** We're adding safety gates:
1. "Does this user own this investment?" ✅
2. "Did they lose too much money today?" ✅  
3. "Is this position size okay?" ✅
4. "Is the account frozen/paused?" ✅

Only AFTER all checks pass, the money moves.  
**Real Life Example:** It's like asking your parents 4 questions before letting you take money from the family safe—and they have to say YES to all 4 questions.  
**Status:** ✅ Framework ready | ⏳ Connecting to all money routes soon
