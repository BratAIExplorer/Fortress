## 📊 COMPANY PROFILE

### Business Overview
- **World's 2nd Largest Integrated Zinc Producer** (after China)
- **India's Only Integrated Producer** of Zinc, Lead, and Silver
- **Top 5 Global Silver Producer**
- **Key Subsidiary:** Vedanta Group (64.9% ownership)
- **Government of India:** 29.5% ownership (strategic investor)

### Operations
- **Mines:** Rajpura-Dariba (Rajasthan), Sindesar Khurd (Rajasthan), Rampura-Agucha (Madhya Pradesh)
- **Smelters:** Multiple state-of-the-art facilities in India
- **Refineries:** Silver, Zinc, Lead refining capacity
- **Employees:** ~23,000+
- **Production Capacity:** ~1 million tonnes Zinc + 300,000 tonnes Lead annually

### Market Position
- **Domestic Market Share:** ~60% of India's zinc demand
- **Export Markets:** Global presence (Europe, Asia, Americas)
- **Commodity Exposure:** Zinc (60%), Lead (20%), Silver (15%), Cadmium (5%)
