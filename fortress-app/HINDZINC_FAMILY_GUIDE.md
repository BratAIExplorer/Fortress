## 📌 WHAT IS HINDUSTAN ZINC?

**Simple Explanation:**
Hindustan Zinc Limited is India's biggest **zinc mining company**. Think of it like this:
- It digs zinc out of the ground (useful metal)
- Sells it to industries who make batteries, galvanized steel, and other products
- Pays you a dividend (bonus) every quarter for owning shares
- This company has been doing this successfully for 50+ years

**Why should you care?**
- ✅ It's run by professionals (Vedanta Group manages it)
- ✅ Government of India is also a major shareholder (29.5%)
- ✅ It's the 2nd largest zinc producer in the WORLD
- ✅ It pays shareholders ₹22.50 per share every year
